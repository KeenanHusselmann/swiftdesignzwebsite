How to add this signature in Gmail

  1. Click "Select Signature" below — the signature box will highlight.
  2. Press Ctrl+C (Windows) or Cmd+C (Mac) to copy it.
  3. Open Gmail → Settings (gear) → See all settings → General → Signature → Create new.
  4. Click inside the signature text area and press Ctrl+V to paste.
  5. Scroll down and click Save Changes.
