How to add this signature in Gmail

  1. Click "Copy Signature" below.
  2. Open Gmail → Settings (gear) → See all settings → General → Signature.
  3. Click Create new, give it a name (e.g. "Swift Designz").
  4. Click inside the empty signature text area and paste (Ctrl+V).
  5. Scroll down and click Save Changes.
