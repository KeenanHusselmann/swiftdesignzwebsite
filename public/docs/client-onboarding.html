Communication Guidelines
Feedback window: Please respond to review requests within 7 business days. Extended silence may pause the project timeline.
Changes: All change requests must be submitted in writing (email or Change Request Form SD-CR-2026).
Preferred channel: Email is the official channel. Messaging apps may be used for quick questions only — formal approvals must be via email.
Proxy: If you'll be unavailable for 7+ business days, please nominate a proxy who can approve decisions on your behalf.
